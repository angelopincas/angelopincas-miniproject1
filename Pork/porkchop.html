Pork Chops

    Ingredients

  • 6 center cut pork loin rib chops, bone-in and about ½-inch thick
  • ¼ teaspoon pepper
  • ½ cup soy sauce
  • ¼ cup calamansi juice
  • 4 cloves garlic, peeled and minced
  • canola oil

    Instruction

  1. Rinse the pork chops and pat dry. Season with pepper to taste.
  2. In a bowl, combine soy sauce, calamansi juice, and garlic.
  3. Add pork chops, massaging marinade onto meat. Cover with film (or transfer into a resealable bag) and marinate in refrigerator for at least 30 minutes or up to 4 hours.
  4. In a pan over medium-high heat, heat oil until hot but not smoking.
  5. Drain pork chops well, discarding marinade.
  6. Add to hot oil in a single layer and cook for about 2 to 3 minutes or until nicely seared. Turn to the other side and continue to cook for another 1 to 2 minutes or until nicely seared and a thermometer in the center reads 145 F.
  7. Remove from pan and let rest for about 2 to 3 minutes before slicing.
