Pork

Pork Adobo

Pork Adobo is made with succulent pork belly braised in vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and onions. A delicious balance of salty and savory, this hearty stew is the Philippines' national dish for a good reason!

Bicol Express

Bicol Express is pure comfort food! With pork cubes cooked in coconut milk and chili peppers, it's rich, creamy, spicy and delicious. Serve with steamed rice for a hearty and big flavored meal!

Lechon Kawali

Lechon Kawali is a popular Filipino pork dish deep-fried to golden perfection. Golden and crunchy on the outside, moist and flavorful on the inside, it makes a tasty appetizer or main dish.

Pork Tocino

Pork Tocino is a hearty Filipino all-day breakfast served with sunny-side-up eggs and garlic fried rice. Sweet and garlicky, it's sure to be a family favorite.

Pork Chop

Toyomansi Pork Chops are a Filipino-style dish seasoned with soy sauce, calamansi juice, and garlic. They're quick and easy to make yet pack amazing flavor perfect with steamed rice!
